Step 1: Linear Term
$$ z = w \cdot x + b $$
Step 2: Sigmoid (Prediction)
$$ \hat{y} = \sigma(z) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-z}} $$
Step 3: Loss (per point)
$$ L = -[y \ln(\hat{y}) + (1-y) \ln(1-\hat{y})] $$
Step 4: Total Cost
$$ J = \frac{1}{m} \sum_{i=1}^{m} L_i $$
