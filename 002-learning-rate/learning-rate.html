How it works

  • Curve: The "Loss Landscape". We want to find the bottom (minimum loss).
  • Gradient: The slope. Tells us which way is "down".
  • Alpha (α): The step size.
    • Small: Safe but slow.
    • Good: Fast convergence.
    • Large: Overshoots, unstable.
